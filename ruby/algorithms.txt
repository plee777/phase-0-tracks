algorithm-for-using-an-elevator

1. walk up to the door
2. face the door with either of your hand ready to press any of the two buttons
3. press the button (up or down depending on destination)
4. wait until the elevator rings up to the floor. 
5. elevator makes a noise and door opens.
6. step into the elevator and face the elevator keys
7. press the button (if it has not been clicked on already)
8. wait until the door closes.
9. stay in the elevator until your final destination.
10. walk out of the elevator.
